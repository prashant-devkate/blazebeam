Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is your maximum fabrication capacity?
Our facility is capable of handling structures up to 6 tons in a single piece. For larger projects, we work in modular sections that are assembled on-site. Our 200-ton press brake and overhead crane give us significant capacity for heavy-duty work.
Do you offer turnkey project delivery including on-site erection?
Yes. Our turnkey solutions cover the complete project lifecycle — from initial design and engineering through fabrication, shop assembly, testing, delivery, and on-site commissioning. We are accountable for the entire scope.
What materials do you work with?
We work with mild steel (MS), stainless steel (SS — all grades), carbon steel, and aluminium. For exotic alloys and special materials, please contact us to discuss feasibility.
Are your welders certified?
Yes — all 8 of our welders hold certifications per ASME/AWS standards for structural and pressure vessel welding. We maintain records of all welder qualifications and revalidate them as required.
Can you fabricate food-grade and pharmaceutical-grade equipment?
Absolutely. We regularly fabricate hygienic stainless steel equipment for dairy, food processing, and pharmaceutical applications — with full material traceability, electro-polishing options, and documentation packages.
What is your typical lead time?
Lead times depend on project complexity and current workload. For standard fabrication items, we typically quote 2–4 weeks. For full turnkey projects, timelines are agreed upfront with milestone-based project management to ensure on-time delivery.
